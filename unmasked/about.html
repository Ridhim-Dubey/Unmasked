About UNMASKED

UNMASKED is a powerful AI-powered deepfake detection system designed to combat the growing threat of manipulated media. Our mission is to empower individuals and organizations to discern real from fake, fostering a more trustworthy online environment.

We leverage cutting-edge deep learning algorithms trained on massive datasets of real and synthetic images to identify deepfakes with exceptional accuracy. Our user-friendly platform allows for seamless upload and analysis of images, providing instant results and peace of mind.

The UNMASKED Team

UNMASKED is driven by a passionate team of engineering students dedicated to creating a safer online landscape. We combine expertise in artificial intelligence, computer vision, and web development to continuously enhance our technology and user experience.
